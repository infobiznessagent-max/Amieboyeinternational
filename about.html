Our Story

Built on Integrity,
Driven by Results

Amieboye International Limited is a Nigerian company operating within the realms of natural oil and gas marketing, shipping and logistics, freight forwarding, protective agent, ship chandler, consultancy, manpower supply, crew supply & crew change, sourcing and general contracting.

With a focus on oil and gas, maritime, construction, petrochemical industries, food production & manufacturing services, labour contract and government agencies, the company aims to leverage its full potentials to provide high-integrity services in Nigeria and internationally.

By contributing to economic and social growth through project management expertise, technology exchange, and community development initiatives, Amieboye International Limited seeks to achieve its core objectives.

AIL policy is to render on-time, high-quality technical services in a safe environment and at the best price.

Our Mission

To apply our full EPC capability to achieve high-integrity services in Nigeria and internationally, contributing to economic and social growth.

Global Partnerships

We enjoy good business relationships with Ascendance International LLC, Global Trading International LLC and Washington Nationwide Mortgage Corporation.

Meet Our Founder

Dr. Christian Amieboye Abaku Founder & Chief Executive Officer
PhD · FCIHRM · CGHRA

Christian Amieboye Abaku, PhD International Relations is the founder and CEO of AMIEBOYE INTERNATIONAL LIMITED. He is a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Human Resource Management (FCIHRM), Chartered Global HR Analyst (CGHRA), Chartered Human Resource Consultant (CHRC).

He holds a Master of Science (MSc) in International Relations from the University of Port Harcourt, is an Associate Chartered Public Manager (ACPM) of the Chartered Institute of Public Management of Nigeria, and holds a Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Industrial Relations and Personnel Management from Lagos State University.

Christian Abaku has more than twenty years of working experience across different firms and organisations, with inventory and innovative skills gained through roles including:

  • Cargo Agent, Protective Agent & Shipping Agent Fejudam Services Ltd
  • Nigeria Representative Washington Nationwide Mortgage Corporation
  • Human Resource Manager Ollytine Nigeria Limited
  • Freight Forwarding & Documentation East-Line International Limited
