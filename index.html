About Amieboye
International

Amieboye International Limited is a Nigerian company operating within the realms of natural oil and gas marketing, shipping and logistics, freight forwarding, protective agent, ship chandler, consultancy, manpower supply, crew supply & crew change, sourcing and general contracting. With a focus on oil and gas, maritime, construction, petrochemical industries, food production & manufacturing services, labour contract and government agencies.
